This page will collect fixes for common issues, including:

- **A system folder isn't showing up** — usually a missing core.
- **A game won't launch** — ROM-set/version or a missing
  [BIOS](/leaf-docs/guide/bios-and-cores/).
- **Wi-Fi connects but has no internet** — DHCP and security-mode notes.
- **Updates won't download** — connectivity and certificate checks.
- **Device booted to stock instead of Leaf** — what the crash-safety fallback
  means and how to recover.

If you hit something not listed here, please
[open an issue](https://github.com/Utility-Muffin-Research-Kitchen).
